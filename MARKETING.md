- $500 account credit per referred agency that signs up

- Top referrers get featured in case studies

## Distribution Channels (Priority Order)
1. **Cold outreach** (email + SMS): highest conversion for B2B SaaS, 200 prospects/week
2. **Google Ads**: capture active intent ("insurance agency software")
3. **LinkedIn organic**: thought leadership, agency owner audience
4. **Meta Ads**: awareness + retargeting
5. **Insurance association events**: in-person demos, local credibility
6. **Referrals**: activated after first 10 clients provide social proof

## Pricing Strategy Rationale
- $999/mo Starter undercuts AgencyZoom ($150/user x 5 = $750 but no AI)
- $1,499/mo Growth positioned as premium but justified by 541% ROI
- $2,499/mo Enterprise for multi-location, includes dedicated support
- Setup fee ($2,500) waived for annual contracts (incentivize commitment)
- 90-day money-back guarantee reduces risk perception to near-zero
