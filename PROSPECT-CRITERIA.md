# PolicyPilot - Prospect Search Criteria

## Geographic Focus
- **Primary:** Sacramento County, Placer County, El Dorado County
- **Secondary:** Yolo County, San Joaquin County, Solano County
- **Expansion (Month 2+):** Greater Bay Area, Central Valley, Southern California

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Must-Have
- Independent insurance agency (not captive/exclusive)
- 300-2,000 policies in book
- At least 2 producers/agents
- In business 3+ years
- Property & casualty lines (personal or commercial or both)
- Currently using an AMS or spreadsheets

### Strong Signals
- Agency owner 40-60 years old (established, tech-aware but not tech-native)
- Google reviews mention "responsive" or "personal service"
- Website hasn't been updated in 2+ years
- Multiple carriers represented
- Posts about hiring challenges (understaffed = needs automation)
- Active in local insurance associations

### Disqualifiers
- Captive agencies (State Farm, Allstate, Farmers exclusive agents)
- Under 100 policies (too small for ROI)
- Already using AgencyZoom or Indio
- Life-only agencies
- Active M&A

## Search Queries

### Google Maps

- "commercial insurance agency Roseville"

- "insurance broker Elk Grove"

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Title: Insurance Agent OR Insurance Broker OR Agency Owner OR Agency Principal
- Company size: 2-50 employees
- Industry: Insurance
- Location: Sacramento, CA (50-mile radius)
- Exclude: State Farm, Allstate, Farmers, Liberty Mutual, Nationwide

### Industry Directories
- IIABA (Big I) member directory
- California Department of Insurance license lookup
- Trusted Choice agent finder
- Apollo.io: Insurance, 2-50 employees, Sacramento MSA

## Outreach Volume
- Month 1: 200 prospects, 100 emails/week
- Month 2+: 200 emails/week sustained
- Target response rate: 8-12%

## Qualification Questions
1. How many total policies in your book?
2. What AMS do you use?
3. How do you handle renewal tracking?
4. How many policies lapsed in the last 6 months that shouldn't have?
5. Do you have a system for identifying cross-sell opportunities?
6. How long does a new business quote take?
7. What would recovering $50K-$100K in lost commissions mean for your agency?
