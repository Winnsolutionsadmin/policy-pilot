# PolicyPilot Brand Identity

## Name
**PolicyPilot**

## Tagline
"Your Renewals Never Sleep"

## Domain
policypilot.ai

## Brand Essence
PolicyPilot is the AI command center for insurance agencies drowning in renewal tracking, cross-sell blindspots, and slow quoting. One system replaces the spreadsheets, sticky notes, and missed commissions that cost agencies $180K+ annually.

## Color Palette
- Primary Deep Navy: #0f1629
- Secondary Trust Slate: #1e293b
- Accent Shield Blue: #2563eb
- Highlight Commission Gold: #d4a843
- Alert Renewal Red: #ef4444
- Success Bound Green: #10b981
- Text Primary Clean White: #f1f5f9
- Text Muted Slate Gray: #94a3b8

## Logo Concept
Stylized shield shape with an upward-trending flight path through it, forming a subtle "P." The shield represents protection (insurance core), the flight path represents AI autopilot.

## Brand Voice
- Authoritative but not stuffy
- Revenue-focused: every feature ties back to commission dollars
- Urgency without panic
- Respectful of the craft: insurance agents are relationship builders

## Typography
- Headlines: Space Grotesk (bold, geometric, trustworthy)
- Body: Inter (clean, high readability)
- Monospace accents: JetBrains Mono (data displays)

## Competitive Positioning
PolicyPilot vs. manual tracking: 67% fewer missed renewals, 4x cross-sell identification, 73% faster quoting
PolicyPilot vs. generic CRM: purpose-built for insurance workflows
PolicyPilot vs. AgencyZoom/HawkSoft: AI-native, 10x faster setup, no per-user licensing trap
